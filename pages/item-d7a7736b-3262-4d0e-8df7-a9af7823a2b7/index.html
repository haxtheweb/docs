Each faculty exhibit includes at least two videos that exemplify the design guidelines and characteristics discussed in the best practice articles associated with this gallery. The exhibits include more than the videos themselves; the commentary and annotations provided make clear which guidelines have been followed and specifically how they have been followed. Navigate to an exhibit or learn more below.

What is in an exhibit?

  • Brief background information
  • Effectiveness scorecard
  • Commentary on the video as a whole
  • Annotations within the video
  • Link menu to jump to in-video annotations

What does it provide?

  • Briefly explain the rationale and the defining qualities of the approach taken by the lecturer and what makes it unique
  • Show which of the 23 guidelines proposed by the two articles are satisfied in a given video
  • Many qualities are evidenced by the video as a whole, rather than by specific moments in the video, and are therefore discussed separately
