Welcome to the HAX community's Contributor Guide! In this section you will find information to help you figure out how you can bring your unique abilities and interests to the community. Contributions don't have to all be code. You could contribute to HAX core, the ecosystem, organize an event, help with translations, documentation, tutorials, or growing the community! Whether you are new to contributing to the project and community or brand new, you can use the following as entry points into the community!

Contribution Opportunities

  • Browse the "new here" tag in our issue queue, intended for new and first time contributions.
  • Explore the Pillars of our community, each Pillar has issues and details as to what that Pillar means
  • Follow our organization on Github, find repos in the ecosystem to contribute to
  • Join our Discord
  • Use HAX.cloud to try HAX today and share HAX on social media
  • Follow HAX on YouTube, watch tutorials
  • Install the HAX cli to streamline contribution to HAX via webcomponents

Part of Penn State

  • Join HAX The Club on Discord and learn about in person events and meet ups
  • Apply for HAX Lab
  • Write HAX into grants to help support the students and infrastructure
